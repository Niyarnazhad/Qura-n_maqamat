Recitations by Sheikh Yasser Al-Dossari

8 Maqams

1. Maqam Bayati

A warm and emotional maqam, often used in the beginning of recitations.

2. Maqam Hijaz

A spiritual and deep maqam, expressing awe and reverence.

3. Maqam Nahawand

This maqam conveys sadness and humility.

4. Maqam Rast

A majestic maqam, often used to signify strength or stability.

5. Maqam Kurd

The Voice of Reverence

6. Maqam Sega

A maqam known for its deep, nostalgic feeling in Qur'anic recitation.

7. Maqam Ajam

A cheerful and majestic maqam, often used to express joy and celebration.

8. Maqam Saba

A maqam that conveys sorrow, yearning, and deep emotion.
